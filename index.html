What Is Web-Development?

Web-Development is a process when worker develops web in two different ways: Front-End and Back-End.

Front-End Development - means, developing web's interface. it's the part, which users can see. for Front-End Development you need to learn mostly HTML, CSS and JavaScript(A.K.A EchmaScript).

But Developer can be Front-End, Back-End and Full-Stack.

Who Is Full Stack Developer?
Full-Stack Developer is a worker who deals with both Front and Back-End side of the site. Full-Stack Dev can be obtained by years of practice. but if you are a Junior dev you can't get it.
